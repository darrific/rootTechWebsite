Who we are

Root Technologies is an I.T. solutions company based in San Fernando, Trinidad and Tobago We are a group of young professionals, currently attending Naparima College at the Sixth Form level. We are experienced and highly trained, prepared to provide creative and innovative solutions to any of your ICT problems.


Several of our technicians are CompTIA A+ Certified, and have experience in network design for Homes, and Small Businesses. Others are proficient in web design, having done so as a hobby, and at an accredited examination level. Despite our age, we have been working with computers for years, and our combined knowledge surpasses many people in the field. We can provide a fresh take on old problems, apply creativity and innovation into our solutions and show our drive and determination in helping our clients.


We are an equal partnership. There are no employees or employers in our organisation and we collaborate on every job that we take. Members all work on whatever task that their particular skillsets best fit, in the interests of an efficient workflow. We pride ourselves on our work and our customer service is friendly, helpful and prompt.
