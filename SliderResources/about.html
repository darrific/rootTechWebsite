About Us


Our Story


Kelvin Ali has been a travel agent since the 1980's. He realized that he would better serve his growing clientele if he had his own business. As a result he founded Kelvin Cruise and Travel Service in 2011. The business is entirely owned and operated by his family, whom he considers his 'team mates'. At the helm of this vastly expanding agency, Kelvin strives to always please the customer, in keeping with his strong moral convictions and his personal motto: "We don't sell airline tickets, we sell service".
Today, Kelvin Cruise and Travel Service is one of the fastest growing small business in Trinidad, with an ever expanding client base. We invite you to join the family!
